# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Everyday consumers shopping online. They arrive with intent to browse or buy, on desktop or mobile, in typical ambient lighting. Primary job: find a product, evaluate it quickly, and complete a purchase with confidence.

## Product Purpose

ShopFlow is a consumer e-commerce app for browsing and ordering products. Success means a shopper can discover, evaluate, and purchase a product without friction or doubt.

## Brand Personality

Bold, direct, trustworthy. The feel of Shopify-powered storefronts: confident commerce, clean hierarchy, strong calls to action. Never timid, never cluttered.

## Anti-references

- AliExpress: visual noise, overcrowded grids, discount-banner overload, cheap feel
- Generic marketplace sameness: anonymous white pages with zero brand character

## Design Principles

1. **Confidence over clutter** — every screen is intentional and curated, nothing competes for attention unnecessarily
2. **Commerce-first hierarchy** — UI decisions serve the purchase journey; price and CTA are always the loudest elements on a card
3. **Bold without noise** — strong contrast and scale do the work; decorative elements earn their place
4. **Trust through restraint** — generous white space and clear affordances make buyers feel safe to act

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. Standard contrast ratios, keyboard navigability, no motion assumed by default.
